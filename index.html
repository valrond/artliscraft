about L'ArtLis

The paintings and drawings presented in the gallery are mostly in the styles of surrealism and fantastic realism. The artist did not come to this style immediately. In 2010, Karyna graduated from the architectural academy where she was already skilled in drawing. In 2016, she intensively studied the art of painting and drawing at the “painting studio” in Grenoble. Her mentors were Catherine Harley and Pierre de Michelis. And only in 2021 did Karyna find her own style, thanks to the experience gained earlier and acquaintance with the works of such artists as: Salvador Dalí , Hans Ruedi Giger, Frida Kahlo, which served as a source of inspiration.
